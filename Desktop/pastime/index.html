My first and longest occuring pastime.

I have been playing video games for a large majority of my life. I was actually exposed to the activity during my wee years in pre-Kindergarten. I don't remember my exact first game I ever played, but it could have been either a Mario game or Donkey Kong Country or Pokemon. My first game that I ever bought with my own money however, was Final Fantasy 9. I currently own most consoles after the NES, my current favorites being the PC, PS2 and PlayStation 4. However I will admit that there is a slight bias towards Sony since I grew up with their system.

Ever since then, it's been a very slippery and expensive slope.
