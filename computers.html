We're surrounded by computers and it's not hard to build your own Rig.

It's literally just legos for adults. Basic things to look out for when building your own computer:

  1. Buy what you comfortably afford!
  2. Have a proper budget in place!
    • You can build a computer for less than $500
    • More powerful rigs that are future-proof can range up closer to $1000+
    • If a newer component is about to released soon, hold off until it is out
  3. Make sure all your parts are compatible with each other! Sites like PC Part Picker are great to use and eliminate a lot of the headache.
  4. Future-proof when you can! General rule of future-proofing is if a component is not moving very quickly, then you can future-proof.
    • Currently, power supplies are moving slow so you're free to future-proof!
  5. Buy what you can comportably afford!
